Overview

Lone Sol is my 3rd team project with a large quantity of people from various disciplines in university and can be played here!

Development spanned around 9 months starting during september 2022 ending on may 2023.

I was 1 of 2 programmers in this project and used Unreal Engine 5. I used Unreal Blueprints (Visual scripting system) and introduced many features into this project, more notably the player controller and the companion controller.
