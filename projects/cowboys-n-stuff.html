Overview

Cowboys n' Stuff is my first published game which can be found on steam here!

Began development on my own in 2019 and published Cowboys n' Stuff just before my first year of university in 2020.

One of my favourite mechanics when developing this game has to be the endless system. As this was my first big project it was fun to try figure out how to make a mathematical system which gets progressively difficult the longer you play.
